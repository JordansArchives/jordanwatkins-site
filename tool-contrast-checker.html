WCAG 2.1 (Web Content Accessibility Guidelines) defines minimum contrast ratios to ensure text is readable for people with visual impairments, including color vision deficiencies.

AA Normal (4.5:1) — Minimum for body text under 18pt (or 14pt bold). Required for most web content.

AA Large (3:1) — Minimum for large text: 18pt+ regular weight, or 14pt+ bold.

AAA Normal (7:1) — Enhanced contrast for body text. Recommended for long-form reading.

AAA Large (4.5:1) — Enhanced contrast for large text. The highest accessibility standard.
